Our Philosophy

Perfectly
imperfect, on purpose.

Factory plates are clones. Ours carry throwing rings, glaze pools, and the slight lean of a human hand — small records of the eleven days each piece spends in the studio. We believe a table set with handmade things changes how a meal feels.

No two alike

Every piece is individually thrown and glazed. Sets are matched by family, never by clone.

Material honesty

Local stoneware clay, mineral glazes mixed in-house, unglazed foot rings you can touch.

Built for decades

Vitrified at 1280°C — dishwasher, microwave, and toddler-proof. Chips replaced free for a year.

Good to know

Before you order

Yes — every piece is vitrified at 1280°C, which makes the body non-porous and safe for dishwashers, microwaves, and ovens up to 200°C. Avoid sudden temperature shocks, like moving a dish from freezer to hot oven.

Closely, but never identically. Reactive glazes pool and break differently on every piece, and forms vary by a few millimetres. We match sets by glaze batch so they belong together — variation is part of what you're buying.

A standard custom dinner set takes 4–6 weeks: one week of sampling, your approval, then production across two firing cycles. Restaurant lines of 100+ pieces typically take 8–10 weeks.

Every shipment is insured. Send us a photo within 48 hours of delivery and we'll fire a replacement in the next kiln load, free of charge. Chips from normal use are also replaced free within the first year.
