Our Methods

The life cycle of a KlinCraft piece.

01

Wedging

Preparing the raw riverbed clay by hand to remove air bubbles and ensure a density.

02

Throwing

Centering the clay on the wheel and using tactile pressure to pull the walls into form.

03

Glazing

Dipping each piece in our proprietary mineral-based glazes to achieve depth and color.

04

Kiln Firing

A 24-hour cycle reaching 1280°C to vitrify the stone, ensuring life-long durability.
