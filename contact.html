Frequently asked

For custom dinnerware sets, our standard lead time is 4 to 6 weeks. Because every piece is hand-thrown, trimmed, and fired in small batches, we cannot rush the drying and firing process without risking the integrity of the ceramics. Wholesale restaurant orders typically take 8 to 10 weeks depending on volume.

Currently, we only ship across India via our secure courier partners. Ceramics are heavy and fragile, making international shipping highly expensive and risky. If you are visiting India and would like to carry pieces back, we provide specialized flight-safe packaging.

Yes! All KlinCraft pieces are fired to 1280°C, making them fully vitrified stoneware. They are incredibly durable, 100% food-safe, and perfectly happy in both the microwave and the dishwasher. We do recommend avoiding extreme thermal shocks (e.g., moving a bowl directly from the freezer to a hot oven).
